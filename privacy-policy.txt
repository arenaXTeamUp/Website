
# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** May 14, 2026

Welcome to ArenaX.

This Privacy Policy explains how ArenaX (“we,” “our,” or “us”) collects, uses, stores, and protects user information when you use the ArenaX mobile application and related services.

By using ArenaX, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

# 1. Information We Collect

## a) Account Information

When users create an account, we may collect:

* Full name or username
* Email address
* Phone number
* Profile photo
* Team information
* Social profile links (optional)

## b) Tournament & Community Information

We may collect:

* Tournament participation history
* Team details
* Match results
* Leaderboard points and rankings
* User-generated tournament content
* Messages or reports submitted to us

## c) Device & Technical Information

We may automatically collect:

* Device model
* Operating system version
* App version
* IP address
* Device identifiers
* Crash logs and diagnostics
* Usage analytics

## d) Location Information

ArenaX may collect approximate or precise location data when users:

* Search nearby tournaments
* Use location radius filters
* Open tournament venues in maps

Location access is only used to improve tournament discovery and navigation features.
